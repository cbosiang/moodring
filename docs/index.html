What is IC?
IC (Information Coefficient) measures how well this indicator predicts future returns. Range: -1 to +1.
Our IC is NEGATIVE — this is good for a contrarian indicator. It means: the more greedy retail investors are, the worse future returns tend to be.

About
MoodRing is a contrarian sentiment indicator covering US, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, and Europe. It scores retail investor sentiment daily using VIX, RSI, margin balance, and institutional fund flows, then compares today's reading against 16 years of historical data (2010–2026) to show what forward returns looked like under similar conditions.
